ALGORITHMIC INTELLIGENCE

The Brain Behind
Perfect Cooking

Sophisticated computer vision and machine learning deliver sub-degree accuracy and intelligent guidance.

01

Visual Processing Pipeline

Every frame undergoes multi-stage processing: noise reduction, perspective correction, and segmentation. U-Net architecture identifies food regions with 94% accuracy. ResNet-18 extracts features tracking browning and texture changes over time.

02

Temperature Estimation

Without infrared sensors, CookSense uses visual cues: RGB color correlates with Maillard reactions, steam patterns indicate heat gradients, and physics-based modeling estimates internal temperature with ±1-2°C accuracy.

03

Browning Detection

L*a*b* color space tracks lightness reduction. Texture analysis quantifies crust development. Edge detection measures surface definition. Combined, these give you a precise browning percentage in real-time.

04

Predictive Timing

LSTM networks forecast when food reaches target state based on historical patterns. Context-aware alerts prevent fatigue while ensuring critical notifications. The system learns optimal timing for your cooking style.
